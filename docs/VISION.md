# Exercise Director

## Vision

Exercise Director exists to give Exercise Directors confidence that they are prepared to meet the objectives and requirements of an exercise, and to help organisations become stronger through continuous improvement.

## Our Purpose

Exercises are not run simply to complete a Master Events List.

Exercises are run to test people, processes, plans and decision-making.

Exercise Director exists to help organisations prepare professionally, deliver confidently, review objectively and improve continuously.

Technology is not the goal.

Better prepared organisations are.

## Better prepared today. Stronger tomorrow.

This is not simply a slogan.

It is our promise.

Every exercise should leave an organisation better prepared than it was before.

Every lesson should lead to improvement.

Every improvement should make the next exercise stronger.

## The Exercise Director Method

### Prepare

Am I prepared to meet the objectives and requirements of this exercise?

Preparation includes:

- Objectives
- Outcomes
- Master Events List
- Injects
- Resources
- Facilitators
- Documentation
- Participants

### Assure

Can I demonstrate that I am prepared?

Exercise Director provides assurance by validating that the exercise has been prepared to the required standard.

Confidence should be supported by evidence, not assumption.

### Deliver

Run the exercise professionally.

Exercise Director supports directing staff through:

- Master Events List
- Inject details
- Lifecycle management
- Activity logging
- Responses
- Decisions
- Situational awareness

The software records facts.

People provide judgement.

### Review

What happened?

The review stage compares objectives against evidence.

The software records what happened.

The directing staff decide what it means.

### Improve

Every exercise should leave an organisation stronger.

Exercise Director captures:

- Lessons identified
- Improvement actions
- Owners
- Due dates
- Evidence of completion

The goal is not simply to identify lessons.

The goal is organisational improvement.

## Design Principles

Exercise Director will:

- Speak the language of exercise professionals
- Record facts, not opinions
- Keep the Master Events List at the heart of the exercise
- Present one source of truth through multiple views
- Support professional decision-making
- Build confidence before an exercise begins
- Promote continuous organisational improvement

## Every Feature Must Answer One Question

Does this help users:

- Prepare?
- Assure?
- Deliver?
- Review?
- Improve?

If not, it probably does not belong.
